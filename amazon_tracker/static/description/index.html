Amazon Tracker

The Amazon Product Tracking module, is a powerful tool that can help you to improve the efficiency and accuracy of your Amazon business.

In this module, you have the ability to update all the products or some products at one time, in two steps.

Short tutorial video about how to update products in two ways.

By tracking the products you can see the history of every product, like price, rating, how many reviews, etc.

The ability to see products in List or Kanban view in Odoo is a powerful feature that can help you to manage your products more efficiently.
